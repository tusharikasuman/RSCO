Career Counselling Solutions - RSCO

Unleash your potential with our bespoke career counselling solutions. We help you align your passion with your profession.

How do we help in Career Counselling?

  • Helps students and professionals understand their skills, interests, and goals.
  • Provides guidance on the right career, education opportunities, and skill development.
  • Support in setting realistic goals and achieving long-term success.

Our 6 Dimensional Assessment

  • Career Interests (RIASEC)
  • Personality (Myers-Briggs)
  • Skills & Abilities
  • Core Motivators
  • Your Unique Learning Style
  • Emotional Quotient (EQ)

Our Proven Process

  1. Pre-Counseling: 30-minute session with you and your parents to understand expectations.
  2. Psychometric Test: Based on the theories of Carl Jung, John Holland, & Myers-Briggs.
  3. Counseling Session: In-depth sessions with a detailed 20+ page report.
  4. Individual Execution Plan: A clear roadmap covering education, improvements, and goals.
