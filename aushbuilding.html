AUSBILDUNG PROGRAM by RSCO

Build a global career with zero tuition fees and earn while you learn!

🔹 Program Overview

  • Duration: 3 years
  • Type: Paid Vocational Training (Ausbildung)
  • Location: Germany
  • Perks: Earn a monthly stipend (1000 euro per month min) while gaining practical experience and in-demand skills

📚 Ausbildung Fields Available

  • Nursing
  • Sales & Marketing
  • IT
  • Retail
  • Engineering
  • Hospitality

💼 Key Benefits

  • ✅ Zero Tuition Fee
  • ✅ Attractive Monthly Stipend
  • ✅ Gain Practical Work Experience
  • ✅ Learn In-Demand Skills

🎯 Eligibility Criteria

  • 12th Pass (Minimum 55%)
  • Age between 18–28
  • German Language Proficiency – B2 Level

🎓 Post-Program Benefits

  • 💶 High Salaries – up to €4,000/month
  • 🇩🇪 Pathway to German Permanent Residency
  • 👨‍👩‍👧 Family Visa Options
  • 🎒 Free Education for Children
  • 💼 Spouse can work in Germany
